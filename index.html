GOAT MAYHEM Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Digital Nova Entertainments ("we", "our", or "us") operates the mobile game GOAT MAYHEM. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when you use our game.

Information We Collect

GOAT MAYHEM does not require account registration.

Third-party services used within the game may automatically collect information such as:

  • Device information
  • Operating system version
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Crash reports
  • Performance diagnostics
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)

This information is collected only through trusted third-party services.

Advertising

GOAT MAYHEM displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect advertising identifiers and other information to provide and improve advertisements.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics

Google services may collect anonymous usage information including:

  • Session length
  • Device type
  • Crash reports
  • Performance information

This information helps improve game stability and performance.

Saved Game Data

GOAT MAYHEM stores game progress locally on your device, including:

  • Coins
  • Gems
  • Unlocked goats
  • Upgrades
  • Best Distance
  • Best Herd Size
  • Other gameplay progress

This information is not shared with us.

Children's Privacy

GOAT MAYHEM is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
